Intro

My name is Jurgen Fataj. I am a full-time technology professional in the field of Databases while also pursing a part-time Masters degree in Computer Science. I am orginally from Vlore, Albania and currently based in Boston, Massachusetts. Outside of coding, I enjoy biking, travelling, playing soccer and spending time at the beach. I am an avid sports fan, supporting the Boston Patriots, Boston Celtics and Arsenal FC.

My professional journey started when I was still very young and joined the Mathematics club in my school. Through that experience, I developed an intuition for creatively solving challenging problems in Math Olympics. I pursued that interest even further in college by going the applied route, majoring in Physics and Statistics.

However, it was not until I started working with Data and Technology that I realized the passion to solve problems and identify patterns was the only constant across the different fields I had been studying. That lead me to pursue a graduate degree in Computer Science to unveil the potential of Technology to create change.
